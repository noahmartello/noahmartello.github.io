Privacy Policy

Blu's Risky Roller (Discord bot) — Last updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Blu's Risky Roller ("the bot") collects when you or a Discord server you belong to uses it, why we collect it, and how you can request its removal.

1. Data We Collect

The bot stores the following data in Google Firebase Firestore:

  • Discord IDs — user IDs, guild (server) IDs, role IDs, and channel IDs.
  • Roll usage counters — how many rolls a user has used per role, and when.
  • Win history — which winnable items (roles or instant wins) a user has won, how many times, and when.
  • Active time-limited role entries — for won roles that auto-expire, the scheduled removal time.
  • Guild configuration — winnable items an admin has configured, drop chances, allowed roles, and channel/server settings.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Message content.
  • User presence, status, or activity.
  • Voice channel activity.
  • Direct messages that are not sent to the bot.
  • Email addresses, real names, or any information outside your public Discord identity.

3. Why We Collect It

All stored data is required for the bot's core function: enforcing per-role roll limits, tracking wins across sessions, running leaderboards, and scheduling time-limited role removals that must survive bot restarts. Data is never used for advertising, sold, or shared with third parties.

4. Where Data Is Stored

Data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud). Access is restricted to the bot service account. Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS.

5. Data Retention

Guild-scoped data is retained while the bot remains a member of the guild. User-scoped data (roll counters, win history) is retained until the user requests deletion or the guild removes the bot. Configuration set by a guild admin persists until an admin removes it via the bot's admin commands.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your personal data (user ID, roll counts, win history) at any time. To do so, contact the operator at the address below with your Discord user ID. Requests are typically processed within 7 days.

Server administrators may remove all guild-scoped data by removing the bot from the server; the operator will purge remaining records on request.

7. Children

The bot is intended for users who meet Discord's minimum age requirement (13, or higher in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from users below that age. If you believe such data has been collected, contact us for deletion.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. Continued use of the bot after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
